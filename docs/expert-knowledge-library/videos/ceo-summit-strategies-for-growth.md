# Strategies for Growth-Mastering Customer Acquisition and Retention

Customer acquisition and retention are crucial for sustainable growth. Join Kate Wolin, an expert in developing tech-enabled health behavior change programs that engage and retain customers to affect population health outcomes, and Michael Marty, who brings decades of experience driving B2B2C customer acquisition and retention across companies including Leap Event Technology, Care.com and Patch.com, as they share insights and practical strategies for building a robust recruiting engine and fostering customer retention.
