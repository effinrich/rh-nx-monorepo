# Partnering with Healthcare Incumbents & Buyer Appetite for Digital Health Solutions

Join Molly Coye, M.D., a digital health trailblazer for over 30 years and Redesign Health Advisor, for a captivating fireside chat with Venture Chair Missy Krasner. As a former Board Member of Aetna with 15 years of active service and the former Chief Innovation Officer for UCLA Health, Molly will discuss buyer appetite for these types of digital health solutions, how to win business and how to position yourself to incumbents. She will also talk about her many lessons learned in scaling these solutions from her decades of experience mentoring CEOs and being inside boardrooms, with companies like Ginger/Headspace, Amedisys, BabyScripts, Hazel Health, and Curai Health. Gain valuable insight into what healthcare leaders are thinking about right now with regard to the next wave of innovation, as well as what risks they are willing to take.
