# Video: How to Get Noticed by Research Analysts and Why it Matters

Marc Albanese, Director of Venture Research at Redesign Health, spent nearly 5 years at CB Insights running the digital health and emerging tech research practices. There, he led market research, content strategy and oversaw the creation of industry reports like CBI Digital Health 150 list and State of Digital Health. Marc discusses what research analysts are, why you should care about them and best practices for getting noticed by them. He also dives into a case study of Digital Health 150.
