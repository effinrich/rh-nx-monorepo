# Video: Series A Fundraising in Today's Market: UpLift Series A & Lessons Learned Fireside Chat

CEO of Uplift Kyle Talcott, Redesign Health Venture Chair Missy Krasner and VP of Strategic Finance Mike Pappas discussed key learnings and takeaways from UpLift's Series A fundraise. This includes the need to go out much earlier and prepare to talk to dozens of firms, to focus less on growth and more on unit economics, and the importance of ensuring board alignment and understanding that a term sheet doesn't mean you've closed the deal.
