# CEO Conversations: Value-Based Care 101 with Syntax Leaders

Rachael Jones, CEO and Co-Founder, and Emily Walker, Chief Analytics Officer of Syntax, lead a session about key information that healthcare CEOs and leaders need to know about value based care. They highlight the significance of value-based healthcare in ensuring profitability and demonstrating value to healthcare partners and investors, and emphasize a data-driven approach and the necessity of transparency in partnerships. They also discuss the challenges and successes in the implementation of value-based care, including the adoption of specific strategies for healthcare sector improvements. Their presentation also underlines the crucial need for early data collection and alignment of care models to effectively meet the demands of health plans and patients within the value-based care framework.
