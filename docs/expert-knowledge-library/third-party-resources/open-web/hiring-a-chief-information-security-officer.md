# Hiring a Chief Information Security Officer

The CISO's role has grown in importance as cybersecurity threats increased. They lead the fight against attacks, manage data security and risks, and report to higher positions in organizations. Effective CISOs enable security, translate threats for all stakeholders, and protect the company's brand in case of breaches. They align risk tolerance with business needs and prioritize secure practices.
