# Outside Counsel Evaluation Form Template

Use this template as a starting place to determine which factors to evaluate your outside counsel by in the early stages of your company. Includes categories such as legal knowledge and performance, business and solution orientation, budgeting, billing, and fiscal accountability, project and time management, communication and responsiveness, values and relationships, and overall satisfaction.
