# PDF Attachment Newsletter Template
If you prefer to attach a separate document to an email to send your newsletter, easily create a branded, fully designed PDF with this template.

Note, while creating a PDF is sometimes the easiest option, an attachment puts your email at higher risk of ending up in your recipients’ “spam” folders. We recommend using the [Email Newsletter Template](https://platform.redesignhealth.com/library/THpfvHLn/module/pqt9EzYW) that embeds directly into the body of the email.
