# Template: OpCo Two-Way NDA for Outside Entities

 Use this template to create a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) — or confidentiality agreement —  between your company and another business or entity individual; it is mutual or two-way.

Always consult your Relationship Manager (RM) and legal counsel if you have questions about how to use a legal template.
