# Messaging Ladder Template

**Instructions**

1. Have your company’s Foundational Brand Identity in hand.

1. Review the framework, then complete the exercise by filling in the “Messaging Guidance” column to help define what feelings and insights your brand’s messaging should convey. Aim for clarity and concision and be compelling with your language.

1. Revisit this tool as a way to refine messaging each time you deliver it, such as in a pitch. It will evolve with your brand.
