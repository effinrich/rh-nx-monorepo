# Template: MSO Compliance Plan (long form)
Use this template to document your company’s comprehensive compliance program if the company is a Management Services Organization (MSO).

During your bounded engagement, work with your Relationship Manager and Redesign Health Legal Point of Contact to customize this template to your company’s needs.

Includes sections on:

* Written policies and procedures

* Compliance officer and committee

* Training and education

* Reporting process

* Disciplinary action

* Audits

* Investigations
