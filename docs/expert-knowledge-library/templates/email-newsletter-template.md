# Email Newsletter Template

With this template, easily create an email newsletter that’s branded, fully designed, and embeds directly into the body of the email (no attachments).

Use it to go beyond a plain text email newsletter in one of three ways:  

* As a PNG file that’s directly embedded into the body of the email.
* As a design guide that’s used to build an HTML version within your CRM (Hubspot or other).
* As a PDF file that’s attached as a separate document to the email (*not advised, as attachments put your email at higher risk of ending up in your recipients’ “spam” folders).
